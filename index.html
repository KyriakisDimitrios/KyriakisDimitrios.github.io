Objective

My full name is Dimitrios Kyriakis and I am a graduate student in Bioinformatics M.Sc. Program, at the University of Crete (UoC) . I hold a B.Sc. degree in Biology, from Faculty of Sciences at Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (AUTH) . You can consult my short resume online, or view a detailed version in PDF format which is available for preview or download. Please feel free to contact me.

What I Do ?

Currently, I am working on my thesis at the Institute of Computer Science (ICS) , at Foundation of Research & Technology (FORTH) in Heraklion, under the supervision of Dr. George Potamias. This work is in collaboration with the Hellenic Centre for Marine Research , (HCMR) and, in particular, with Dr. Costas S. Tsigenopoulos. My thesis title is "Scanning of genetic variants and genetic mapping of phenotypic traits in gilthead seabream through ddRAD sequencing data analysis". The aim is to associate variants with phenotypic traits.
